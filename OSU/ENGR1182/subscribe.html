Business Model

Value Proposition: Porting Porridge, a low-cost high convenience opportunity to those without vehicles to transport goods from stores. Save time and your frozen goods. Protect yourself from fatigue and make your day easier by eliminating the weight on your shoulders.

User Segment: We plan to target college students and public transport users who are always inconvenienced and fatigued from walking with a lot of goods. Our potential user can even extend to people who have their own mean of transportation wit features such as an automatic scanner.

Low-cost, High Convenience: Our research shows that in order to offset the convenience of alternative services and to offset the cost savings that can be provided by walking, out solution must be both lower in cost than purchasing a vehicle and more convenient than walking, carpooling or biking.

Opportunity: Our potential users are those without vehicles focusing specifically on college students. This user can be expanded to those trying to save money and the elderly who can no longer drive. By maximally attained our first goal of low cost, high convenience we hope to finally expand our users to those who have vehicles, but find driving is less convenient than our potential solution.

Transportation Design: Our design will first consider the task, with a design specific to transporting perishable or fragile goods. Secondly, our design seeks to provide maximum convenience to those who use it, by making it easy to use with minimal physical effort. The designing process for the method of transportation will be based off of the feedback directly from our end users and the Users Need’s chart to maximize user satisfaction with the opportunity.
